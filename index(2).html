Our Services

Comprehensive transportation solutions and compliance testing designed for your business needs.

Primary Service

Full Truckload (FTL)

Full Truckload (FTL) offers dedicated dry van or reefer capacity for large shipments, ensuring direct, exclusive transport.

  • Dedicated dry van and refrigerated (reefer) trailers.
  • Best for full or near-full capacity loads.
  • Ideal for time-sensitive, high-value freight.
Flexible Capacity

Less-Than-Truckload (LTL)

Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) provides cost-effective solutions for smaller shipments that don't require a full trailer, maintaining reliable service.

  • Cost-effective partial and shared truckload options.
  • Perfect for recurring small to medium-sized freight.
  • Flexible scheduling to meet your budget and timeline.
Long-haul

Long-haul Truckload

Our over-the-road service covers extensive, long-distance routes throughout the United States.

  • OTR dry van and reefer capacity for regional or coast-to-coast runs.
  • Reliable capacity for recurring long-distance shipments.
  • Solo and team drivers available for strict, time-sensitive delivery.
Compliance Service

California CARB Clean Truck Check

TVM Express offers official CARB Clean Truck Check testing to ensure your fleet complies with California Air Resources Board emissions regulations.

  • Certified Clean Truck Check (HD I/M) testing support.
  • Crucial for trucks operating in California or on West Coast lanes.
  • Simple scheduling: contact dispatch or email to book your appointment.
